🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us — just like a perfectly proofed loaf. This policy explains how we handle your data.

🌾 Our commitment

At Mountain Bread Chronicle, we believe in transparency, sustainability, and respecting your digital footprint. This policy outlines what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. We never sell your data — that’s not how we bake.

In short: We collect only what’s needed to serve you better. Your data stays with us, and you can request deletion anytime.

📋 Information we collect

  • Contact & identity: name, email address, and any details you provide via forms (e.g., newsletter signup, contact messages).
  • Usage data: pages visited, time spent, and referral sources (anonymized, via cookies).
  • Device & browser: IP address, browser type, and operating system (for analytics and security).

🍪 Cookies & tracking

We use minimal, privacy-first cookies to improve your experience. These include:

  • Essential cookies: for basic site functionality.
  • Analytics cookies: to understand how readers interact with our content (via a privacy-respecting service).
  • You can manage your cookie preferences at any time — see our Cookie Policy for details.

🔐 How we protect your data

  • All data is stored securely and access is restricted to essential personnel.
  • We use HTTPS encryption and regularly review our security practices.
  • We never share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

📧 Your rights

  • Access: You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct any inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Ask us to erase your data — we’ll do it promptly.
  • Withdraw consent: Unsubscribe from emails or opt out of analytics at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, simply contact us — we’re here to help.

📅 Updates to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in law or our practices. The latest version will always be posted on this page, with the effective date below. We encourage you to review it periodically.

EFFECTIVE April 1, 2026

Questions? Reach out to our privacy team or use our contact page.
