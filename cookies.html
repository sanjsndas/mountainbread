🍪 Cookies Policy

We use cookies to enhance your experience — like a warm, perfectly baked loaf. Here’s what we use and why.

🌾 Our cookie philosophy

At Mountain Bread Chronicle, we keep things simple and transparent. Cookies help us understand how you interact with our site, remember your preferences, and serve you better. We never use intrusive tracking or sell your data.

Quick bite: We use only essential and performance cookies. No third-party advertising cookies. You’re in control.

📋 What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your actions and preferences (like language or region) so you don’t have to re‑enter them on each visit.

🔐 Your control & consent

When you first visit, we ask for your consent to set non‑essential cookies. You can change your mind anytime:

  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Check your browser’s help section for instructions.
  • Opt‑out: You can disable analytics cookies by adjusting your preferences below (simulated).

We do not use third‑party advertising cookies. We may embed content (like videos) that sets their own cookies, but we have no control over those. We recommend checking the respective third‑party privacy policies.
