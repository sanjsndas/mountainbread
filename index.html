    ⚡ Expert guide: sourdough maintenance

    Step‑by‑step – keep your starter vigorous with these 5 rules. Avoid common mistakes like overfeeding or using chlorinated water.

    MistakeSolutionImpact
    Irregular feedingFixed scheduleStable activity
    Cold waterRoom tempFaster rise
    Over‑discardingKeep 30g baseFlavour depth
    ❓ FAQ: Why does my starter smell like acetone?

    It's hungry – feed it more frequently or reduce hydration slightly. A sign of over‑fermentation.

    ❓ FAQ: Can I use whole grain rye?

    Absolutely. Rye ferments faster and gives a distinct tang. Adjust hydration to 100% for best results.

    📌 pro tip always reserve a spoonful of old starter as a flavour anchor.
